Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

Morning Pantry Balance is an offline single-player puzzle game. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Information Collection

Morning Pantry Balance does not collect personal information. The app does not include account registration, social login, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party purchase services.

Local Game Data

The app may store gameplay progress, unlocked levels, and settings (music, sound, haptics) locally on your device using NSUserDefaults. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Children's Privacy

Morning Pantry Balance is suitable for all ages. Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Internet, Ads, and Purchases

  • No internet connection is required for gameplay.
  • No advertisements are included.
  • No in-app purchases are included.
  • No user tracking is included.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.

Contact

After release, you can contact support through the Morning Pantry Balance App Store product page.
