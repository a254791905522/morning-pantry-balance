About Morning Pantry Balance

Morning Pantry Balance is a breakfast planning puzzle game built with SpriteKit. Each level is a full week: seven breakfasts to plan from a shared pantry of ingredients. Every food item has calories, carbs, protein, and fat values, and each day has a target range the plate must match.

The game spans 50 levels. The first three levels are tutorial weeks with on-screen prompts that guide the next ingredient to tap. From level 4 onward, daily events such as gym workouts, soccer practice, and holiday dinners raise or lower the calorie target, adding a strategic layer to the plan.

Scoring is based on how close the plate lands to the target calories and macros. The pantry is limited, so the game checks ahead: if the remaining stock cannot cover the rest of the week, the level fails. Players can undo, clear the plate, and try again before confirming each day.

How to Play

1

Open the Pantry

Each level starts with a fixed set of ingredients. Review the stock and the seven-day target list before planning.

2

Build the Plate

Tap food cards to add items to the plate. The calorie bar and macro readout update in real time as items are added.

3

Match the Target

Keep total calories inside the day's range and keep carbs, protein, and fat within the macro tolerance.

4

Undo and Clear

Use Undo to remove the last item, or Clear to reset the plate. Adjust freely before confirming the day.

5

Watch the Pantry

The pantry is shared across all seven days. If the remaining stock cannot cover the rest of the week, the level fails.

6

Clear the Week

Complete all seven days with matching targets to clear the level and unlock the next week of planning.

Support

How do I play?

Tap food cards to build a plate that matches the day's calorie and macro targets. The first three levels include on-screen tutorial prompts that guide each step.

Does it need internet?

No. Morning Pantry Balance is fully offline. No account, no ads, no in-app purchases, and no tracking.
