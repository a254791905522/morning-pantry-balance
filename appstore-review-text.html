Morning Pantry Balance is a breakfast planning puzzle game where every level is a full week. You start with a fixed pantry of ingredients and must plan seven breakfasts, one for each day, so that the plate matches the day's calorie target and macro goals for carbs, protein, and fat.

HOW TO PLAY
- Tap food cards to add items to the plate. Each card shows calories and macro values.
- Watch the calorie bar and macro readout update in real time as you build the plate.
- Use Undo to remove the last item or Clear to reset the plate before confirming.
- Tap Confirm when the plate is inside the target range for calories and macros.
- Complete all seven days to clear the level and unlock the next week.

LEVELS AND EVENTS
- 50 levels, each a full 7-day week of breakfast planning.
- Levels 1-3 are tutorial weeks with on-screen prompts highlighting the next food to tap.
- From level 4, daily events such as Gym Workout, Weekend Hike, Soccer Practice, and Holiday Dinner raise or lower the day's calorie target.
- Tolerance tightens as levels progress, demanding more precise planning.

PANTRY STRATEGY
- The pantry is shared across all seven days. Once an item is used, it is gone for the week.
- Fruits rotate each day, so the same fruit is not always available.
- Before confirming a day, the game checks that the remaining pantry can still cover the rest of the week. If not, the week fails.
- Plan ahead: every item should fit the 7-day nutrition plan.

FEATURES
- 14 food items with real calorie and macro values.
- 50 levels with progressive difficulty and daily events.
- Tutorial prompts for the first three levels.
- Undo and Clear tools for flexible planning.
- Music, sound, and haptics toggles in Settings.
- Level select screen with pagination.
- Progress saved locally on the device.

OFFLINE AND PRIVATE
- No internet connection required.
- No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions.
- No account, no analytics, no tracking.
- All progress and settings stored locally on the device.
